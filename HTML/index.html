We are a film production company committed to bring the best stories to the big screen. At +58 films, we firmly believe that movies have the power to shape the way we think and interact with the world. Therefore, creating content that adds value is the goal behind each production.

The Weight of The Air (original title: El Peso del Aire) is a feature film that will take place in two main locations: the spectacular city of Buenos Aires, Argentina and the breathtaking Lake Di Garda, Italy. It tells the story of a millionaire architect who is forced to redefine happiness after losing it all.

Genre: Drama

Estimated running time: 1h 40min

Status: Pre-production

Filming is expected to begin in the summer of 2018

In Theaters 2019

For You (original title: Para Ti) follows a young boy in his attempt to fill the hole that his mother’s death left in his life. The main location for this feature film will be Caracas, Venezuela.

Genre: Drama

Estimated running time: 1h 45min

Status: Pre-production

Filming will take place early 2019

In Theaters 2019
